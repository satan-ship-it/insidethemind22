Introduction

Nature & Process of
Psychotherapy

A voluntary, systematic relationship aimed at modifying maladaptive behaviors and fostering growth. It is not just a cure, but a journey toward self-awareness.

🤝

Voluntary Partnership

A conscious contract between client and therapist to solve problems.

🎓

Systematic Application

Structured use of psychological principles by trained experts.

🌟

The Alliance

The relationship itself is the primary vehicle for healing.

The Therapeutic Alliance

Distinct from friendship, this bond is contractual, limited in duration, and built on specific properties that create a safe space for unburdening.

  • 🛡️
    Strict Confidentiality Trust is the foundation.
  • ❤️
    Empathy vs Sympathy Feeling like the person, not just for them.
  • 👐
    Unconditional Positive Regard Total acceptance without judgment.
